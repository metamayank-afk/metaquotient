About · Beta v3.1

What is MQ?

Core Concept

Think About Thinking

Meta Quotient measures recursive reasoning — predicting what others think you'll think, detecting traps, and reasoning in loops rather than lines.

Why Numbers?

Zero Math Bias

Simple number choices remove cultural and educational bias. Everyone can pick a number. What differs is the depth of reasoning behind the pick.

Speed Warning

Don't Rush

There's no timer, but the system monitors response speed. Rushing 2+ questions triggers a 20% penalty. The best scores come from people who actually thought.

The Vision

AI Benchmarking

Human responses form a baseline dataset. The long-term goal: benchmark how AI models meta-reason compared to humans. You're contributing to that.
